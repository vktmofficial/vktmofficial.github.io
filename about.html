VKTM is a London streetwear brand. No investors. No backing. No second chances. Just the work.

We started with nothing. No studio, no team, no budget — just a name and a reason to make something that felt real. Every piece we drop is a record of where we've been and what we carried to get there.

No restock. Ever. If you miss it, you miss it. That's not a strategy — that's respect for the people who showed up when it mattered. The clothes aren't decorative. They're proof.
