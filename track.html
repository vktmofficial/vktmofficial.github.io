Common Questions.

I paid but didn't get a confirmation email. ▾

Check your spam or junk folder first — Stripe emails sometimes end up there. If it's not there, use the form above with your email address and we'll confirm your payment and resend the receipt manually.

When will my order ship? ▾

Orders are processed within 1–2 business days. Production takes a further 2–3 business days. Once shipped you'll get an automatic tracking email. Total UK delivery time is typically 5–10 business days from your order date.

Can I change or cancel my order? ▾

Orders can be cancelled within 12 hours of purchase before production begins. Email vktmofficial@gmail.com immediately with your order reference. After 12 hours, production will have started and the order cannot be cancelled.

My item arrived damaged or incorrect. ▾

We're sorry about that. Email vktmofficial@gmail.com within 14 days of receiving your order with a photo of the issue and your order reference. We'll arrange a replacement or full refund in line with our Returns Policy.

Do you offer exchanges? ▾

Due to the limited-edition, no-restock nature of VKTM drops, size exchanges are only possible if stock is available. We cannot guarantee it. Make sure you check the sizing guide carefully before ordering.
